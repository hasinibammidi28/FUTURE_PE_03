# Prompt Logic

## Objective

Generate SEO-focused blog content for The Daily Drip Cafe.

## Techniques Used

### Role Prompting
AI was assigned the role of SEO content writer.

### Context Prompting
Business name, location, and keywords were provided.

### Structured Prompting
Specific requirements such as H1, H2, H3 and conclusions were requested.

### Local SEO
Keywords included location-based search terms such as Hyderabad.
